Chili

Description

This award winning chili is the perfect dinner to fill you up and provides all the nutrition you need after a log day. On top of that, you will have this meal completed in less than an hour. From the various beans to the seasonings,there is nothing disappointing about this chili.

Ingredients

  • 5 strips chopped up bacon
  • 1 Diced yello onion
  • 3 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 pount of 90% ground beef
  • 1 Tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 Tbsp ancho chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • black pepper
  • 1 jar each of kindey beans and black beans
  • 14.5 oz can of diced fire roasted tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp worcestershire sauce
  • can of fire roasted tomatoes
  • toppings of your choice like cheese and chips

Instructions

  1. cook bacon in large pot until crisps and remove the bacon
  2. add onion and pepper to pot and cook until soft then add garlic
  3. once fragrant, add beef and break up with spatula
  4. once 50% cooked, add remaining ingredients and stir well
  5. bring to a boil and cook for 2 minutes, stirring it
  6. reduce heat and simmer unconvered for 30 minutes, stirring frequently
  7. add the toppings of your choice!
